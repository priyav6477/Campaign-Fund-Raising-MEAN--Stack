Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept major credit and debit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment methods.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, your payment information is encrypted and processed through a secure payment gateway. We do not store your card details.

Will I receive a receipt for my payment?

Yes, a receipt will be sent to your registered email address after the payment is processed.

How is the donation fee calculated?

A 3% fee is applied to each donation to cover processing costs. The fee is automatically deducted from the donation amount.

I encountered an error during payment. What should I do?

If you encounter an error, please check your internet connection and payment details. If the issue persists, contact our support team.

What should I do if my payment fails?

If your payment fails, please try again or use a different payment method. You can also contact your bank or our support team for assistance.
